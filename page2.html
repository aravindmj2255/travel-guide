Tokyo, Japan

Tokyo, the capital of Japan, is a vibrant city known for its modern skyscrapers, traditional temples, and bustling streets. The Tokyo Tower, shown above, is a famous symbol of the city and offers panoramic views of the metropolis.

From the bright lights of Shibuya Crossing to the historic Asakusa district, Tokyo offers a unique blend of the futuristic and the traditional. Visitors can experience world-class shopping, dine in Michelin-starred restaurants, and explore the rich history and culture of Japan.
