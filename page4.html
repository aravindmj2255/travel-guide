Sydney, Australia

Sydney, Australia's largest city, is known for its stunning harbor and the world-famous Sydney Opera House. The Opera House, shown above, is an architectural masterpiece and a must-see for visitors to the city.

Sydney offers a perfect blend of city life and outdoor activities, from surfing at Bondi Beach to exploring the Royal Botanic Garden. Visitors can also enjoy the vibrant dining scene and take in breathtaking views from the Sydney Harbour Bridge.
