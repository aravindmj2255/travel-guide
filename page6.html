Rome, Italy

Rome, the capital of Italy, is a city filled with history, art, and culture. The Colosseum, shown above, is one of the most famous ancient structures in the world, symbolizing the grandeur of the Roman Empire.

In addition to the Colosseum, Rome is home to other iconic landmarks such as the Vatican City, the Pantheon, and the Trevi Fountain. Visitors can enjoy rich history, stunning architecture, and delicious Italian cuisine.
