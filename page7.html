Istanbul, Turkey

Istanbul, a city that straddles Europe and Asia across the Bosphorus Strait, is known for its rich history and stunning architecture. The Hagia Sophia, shown above, is a magnificent structure that has served as a cathedral, mosque, and now a museum.

Visitors to Istanbul can explore the vibrant Grand Bazaar, experience the beauty of the Blue Mosque, and take a cruise on the Bosphorus. The city offers a unique blend of cultures, cuisines, and historical landmarks that make it a fascinating destination.
