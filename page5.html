Cape Town, South Africa

Cape Town, located at the southern tip of Africa, is known for its stunning natural beauty. The iconic Table Mountain, shown above, offers breathtaking views of the city and the surrounding coastline.

Visitors to Cape Town can explore the vibrant V&A Waterfront, take a boat to Robben Island, or enjoy the beautiful beaches of Camps Bay. The city also offers a rich cultural heritage and a diverse food scene.
